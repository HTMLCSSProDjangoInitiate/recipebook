7 Meals of the Day

Breakfast

The first meal of the day.Usually around 6am-9am.

Brunch

an early morning meal that replaces breakfast and lunch. (informal)

elevenses

a snack, such as coffee and biscuits. about 11 a.m. (BrE, casual)

Lunch

middle of the day meal. Usually at noon or one o'clock.

Tea

Tea is typically served with a light afternoon meal of sandwiches, cakes, etc. about 4:00 PM. It is also referred to as afternoon tea (primarily in BrE). Around 6 o'clock (BrE), a cooked evening meal is also referred to as tea.

supper

A quick or relaxed evening meal. 6 to 7 o'clock.

Dinner

The primary meal of the day, usually eaten in the afternoon or evening. The term "dinner" is typically used to refer to an evening meal, typically between 7 and 9 p.m.
